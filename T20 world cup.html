T20 Cricket World Cup Analytics

In this project we take T20 World Cup 2021 data from ESPNCricInfo and apply Data Cleaning in Python and created a interactive dashboard in Power BI using DAX measures

The first page of the visualization offers an in-depth player analysis, presenting a comprehensive selection of players categorized into distinct roles, including power hitters and anchors. A meticulously curated table lists players sorted according to various performance parameters, facilitating an informed decision-making process for team selection. Additionally, a range of dynamic charts vividly illustrates individual player statistics, enabling stakeholders to gain valuable insights into player performance trends and strengths. This meticulously designed interface serves as a robust foundation for strategizing and assembling the optimal playing 11, ensuring a competitive edge in the T20 Cricket World Cup 2020

The second page of the portfolio showcases a curated list of the best-selected players. This interactive feature allows users to seamlessly replace players from the provided list on the left side according to their specific requirements and preferences. Whether seeking to tailor a team composition for strategic purposes or exploring alternative player combinations, this functionality offers flexibility and customization. With a user-friendly interface and intuitive design, individuals can effortlessly navigate through the selection process, ensuring optimal team configuration tailored to their needs. Whether for cricket enthusiasts, team managers, or analysts, this dynamic feature enhances the portfolio's utility, empowering users to make informed decisions and explore various player combinations with ease
